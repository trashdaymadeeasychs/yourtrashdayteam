Questions, answered

Your Trash Day Team, explained clearly.

Everything you need to know about the combined weekly bin valet from Trash Day Made Easy and the monthly bin washing from The Bin Boy — how it works, what it costs, and how billing and cancellation work.

About the Combined Service

Two established Charleston companies, one simple weekly service.

Your Trash Day Team is a combined service from two established Charleston companies. Trash Day Made Easy handles the weekly bin roll-out and roll-in, and The Bin Boy handles the monthly bin washing. You get one simple weekly routine and one flat monthly price for homes, businesses, and properties.

Trash Day Made Easy runs the weekly valet: rolling your bins out before pickup and bringing them back after collection. The Bin Boy runs the monthly bin washing. Both are local crews, and the two services are billed together under one $70 per month plan.

Valet: Trash Day Made Easy · Washing: The Bin Boy

Your Trash Day Team serves the Charleston, South Carolina area and surrounding Lowcountry communities, including North Charleston, West Ashley, Mount Pleasant, Summerville, James Island, and Daniel Island. If you are not sure whether we reach your address, call 843-955-3132 and we will confirm.

Yes. The service is available to residential homeowners and renters, businesses, and property management companies. You choose your customer type when you sign up.

Weekly Bin Roll-Out and Roll-In

Handled by Trash Day Made Easy.

On your municipal trash day, Trash Day Made Easy pulls your bins from their storage spot to the curb before the collection truck arrives, then returns them to their spot after pickup. You do not have to move anything yourself.

Handled by Trash Day Made Easy

No. Trash Day Made Easy accesses your bins from the storage location you provide, moves them to the curb, and returns them after collection, so you do not need to be home.

Weekly valet service continues in all weather conditions, including rain, wind, cold, and heat.

We service standard residential trash and recycling bins provided by your municipality. Tell us how many bins are at each property when you sign up.

Monthly Bin Washing

Handled by The Bin Boy.

Once a month, The Bin Boy cleans your bins using high-pressure hot water at around 200 degrees to break down buildup, kill bacteria, and cut odor. Your bins are washed and returned.

Handled by The Bin Boy

The Bin Boy uses a biodegradable, non-toxic, odor-neutralizing solution that is safe for children, pets, and your garden, and it washes away cleanly.

Scheduling and Service Days

How your weekly and monthly visits are timed.

The weekly valet is timed to the municipal trash pickup day you select at signup. The monthly bin washing is scheduled by The Bin Boy each month. If you set up recycling, you also tell us the recycling pickup day so we can match the county schedule.

When you submit the signup form, your account is saved as Pending Approval and no charge is made that day. We review the service address and confirm details. Once your account is approved, service begins and billing starts on the 1st of the month.

Property Access and Bin Placement

Getting to your bins and putting them back.

We access your bins from the storage location you describe at signup, such as a side yard, backdoor, or garage area. You can add a gate code or access notes on the signup form. Bins are returned to their storage spot after collection and after washing.

Holidays and Collection Delays

When the municipal schedule shifts.

If your municipal collection is delayed because of a holiday, the weekly valet schedule adjusts to match, and any schedule change is communicated in advance.

Billing and Payment

One flat monthly price, handled securely.

The combined service is $70 per month per property. That includes weekly bin roll-out and roll-in from Trash Day Made Easy and monthly bin washing from The Bin Boy.

There is no charge on the day you sign up. Your card is stored securely with Stripe, and after your account is approved you are auto-billed $70 per property on the 1st of each month.

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover, all processed securely through Stripe.

Yes. Card details are handled by Stripe and are never stored on this website. Only safe references such as the card brand and last four digits are kept for recurring billing.

Pausing or Canceling Service

No contract, no penalty.

No. The service is month-to-month with no long-term contract.

Yes. You can ask us to pause or cancel your service at any time. Service continues until you ask us to stop.

Residential and Business Properties

One home or a whole portfolio.

Yes. You can add multiple properties on the signup form, and each property is $70 per month. Property management companies and portfolio owners are welcome.
